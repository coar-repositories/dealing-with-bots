The effects of increased usage on your repository platform can often be mitigated by upgrading the underlying infrastructure. This may include:
- Increasing server hardware resources (CPU, RAM, disk space)
- Upgrading network bandwidth
- Implementing load balancing solutions
- Moving to cloud-based infrastructure for better scalability
